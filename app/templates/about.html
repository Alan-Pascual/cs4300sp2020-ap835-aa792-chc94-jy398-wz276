FAQ and Website Info




Due to current world events, there have been greater demands for online entertainment. Steam has recently reached a record high for concurrent users; however, there are only so many games on Steam and many gamers may seek to broaden their horizons by watching Japanese animation (anime). But many people might find it difficult to narrow down what anime to watch due to its wide variety of genres and styles. Thus, the goal of our application is to recommend anime to people based on their Steam gaming preferences. By giving our system a game title from Steam, it will return a list of recommended anime based on the similarity in reviews and tags. Each recommendation will also display the general details, trailer video, and MAL link for each suggestion.





When looking at our results you might spot that we assign different colors for anime rating and similarity score based on how high the value is. Similar to most reviewing platforms, we use green for the best, orange for mediocre, and red for anything we would not really recommend. For specific information, our thresholds for anime rating are >7.5 green, >6 for orange and red for anything below. For similarity score, we use >80 green, >50 orange and red for the rest.
